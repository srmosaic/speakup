The School of Thought

The most successful people seem to have an innate charisma. They captivate their audiences when they speak and they get support when they ask for it. You’ve got the same power to charm and "Shway", all you need now is to just feel confident about your delivery.

Started initially as a program to help kids communicate effectively and confidently in 2010 at Plainsboro Recreation Center. The program showed immeasurable benefits in students' professional and personal lives and has grown manifolds since then in the past years. Following such success, we decided to take our pogram to next step and design our public speaking workshops series to go far beyond any typical speaker coaching. With a focus on helping you discover your core strengths and develop the traits of the world’s most captivating speakers, to will master values such as dynamism, comfort, & empathy.


21st Century + Decades of Research

Benefits of being a vocational and all-rounded public speaker is enormus, and we want to make sure you get the best of it. That's why we not only base our workshops and classes, on some of the most renowned and well established schools of thoughts and academic research, but we also incorporate our instructors years of experience from different fields with new media trends in presentation and business practices.
