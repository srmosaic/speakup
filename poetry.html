About the Workshop

Spoken Poetry is an innovative program that encourages students and adults to learn the craft of speaking and presenting the poem (self-written or poems written by someone else) with more expressions and in the right manner. It incorporates the dynamic aspects of slam poetry, spoken word, and theater into English and drama classes. This workshop is designed mainly to help you to perform your writing and to recite it in a better way in front of an audience.

Following modules will be focus of this workshop

  • Articulation & Pronunciation
  • Voice Modulation
  • Poetic Expressions
  • Physical Presence
