Inspire Speaker Series

The Inspire speaker series project is a SR Mosaic led Public Speaking series that inspires and lays down the importance of public speaking. This series shall promote speakers from different walks of the community and profession. The speakers shall relate their own experiences and the need for soft skills. The vesatality of themes covered here range from Confidence Build-up, Presentation Skills to Stage Performance Symposiums enabling participants to gain on-site exposure to various methods involved in public speaking.

“APPLY STORY TO YOUR TALK”

Turn your “meh” messages into persuasive presentations that really mean something to your audience. We’ll teach you to apply lessons from story and cinema and structure your presentation to grab and keep your audience’s attention.

“TURN DATA INTO STORY”

Tap into the emotional influence your data can have by communicating it through a story framework. Advance internal initiatives and bond customers to your brand by humanizing data.


“ENHANCE YOUR TALK WITH STORYTELLING AND DESIGN”

When you present visual stories, people are more likely to understand your ideas and support you. Harness the combined power of narrative and design to connect with and transform your audience.

“TURN IDEAS INTO VISUALS”

This class will teach you to apply visual thinking to your presentations and help people see what you’re saying. Clarify and amplify your message by deploying diagrams, charts, and visual elements with ease—even if you’re not a designer.

“PRESENT LIKE A PRO”

This public speaking class will teach you to be more comfortable, dynamic, and empathetic in front of an audience. You’ll learn to ease your nerves, modulate your voice, and capitalize on the moments you really want people to remember.
